Deleting a Product

 

To delete a Product, select the product and click on button on the Products screen.

 

See the Figure 4.4.

 

One message box is displayed asking-"Do you want to delete the selected product?". Click "yes" to delete the selected product. After deleting the product successfully, the updated Products list is displayed. See Figure4.5

 

Note that a particular product can be deleted only when there are no registrations exists for that product.
